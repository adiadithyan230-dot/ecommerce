Prediction Logic

Our forecasting engine analyzes your historical transaction data and groups it by month to identify underlying sales trends.

We then apply a Linear Regression algorithm from scikit-learn to map these trends against a time index. This allows the system to project the trajectory of your revenue for the next 3 months.

Additionally, the engine identifies your historically best-selling product and top-performing category, predicting their specific demand trajectories using the same regression techniques.
